Welcome to Big Belly!

In a hurry? No worries! Zip in and out in a flash! Enjoy a mouth-watering burger (as well as other delicious menu items) from the best burger joint in all the multiverse. We started from humble roots: after a cataclysmic explosion in downtown Central City, Big Belly founders Mike and Michelina wanted to spread joy to counter the chaos in the community. In 2010, they answered this call with Big Belly Burger. So come on down, we'd love to serve you! - We're across from CC Jitters and the Central City Police - so bring your coffee, bring your speeding ticket, and most importantly: bring your appetite.
